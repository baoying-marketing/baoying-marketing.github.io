Ying Bao

Assistant Professor of Marketing

Gies College of Business, University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign

My research pairs causal inference with economic theory and game theory to study digital platforms — how quality disclosure, content moderation, and commission design shape what firms and consumers do. A second line of work examines food marketing and consumer well-being, from consumption tracking to the link between obesity and food-tax policy.
